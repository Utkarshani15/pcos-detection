Recommended Exercises for PCOS

Monday - Cardio

  • 30-minute brisk walking
  • 15-minute jump rope
  • Cool-down stretching

Tuesday - Strength Training

  • Bodyweight squats - 3 sets of 10
  • Dumbbell lunges - 3 sets of 12
  • Push-ups - 3 sets of 10

Wednesday - Yoga & Stretching

  • 20 minutes of yoga (poses for hormone balance)
  • Deep breathing exercises

Thursday - HIIT

  • 30 seconds sprint + 1-minute rest (repeat 10x)
  • Jump squats - 3 sets of 15
  • Plank hold - 45 seconds

Friday - Full Body Workout

  • Deadlifts - 3 sets of 10
  • Dumbbell rows - 3 sets of 12
  • Leg raises - 3 sets of 10

Saturday - Light Activity

  • Go for a 45-minute walk
  • Stretching and mobility exercises

Sunday - Rest & Recovery

  • Foam rolling
  • Light yoga
  • Hydration and healthy meal planning
